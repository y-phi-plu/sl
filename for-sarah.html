Official Emoji & Abbreviation Reference Guide (v1.0)

LOL Laughing Out Loud. A positive expression. Not a sign-off.
FR For Real. Expresses sincerity. Unrelated to France.
NGL Not Gonna Lie. A honesty disclaimer before a strong opinion.
IKR I Know, Right? Full agreement. Often used enthusiastically.
SMH Shaking My Head. Mild to moderate disappointment. Not a medical term.
🔥 Fire emoji. Means something is extremely good OR everything is in crisis. Context dependent.
💀 Skull emoji. Extremely bad situation, OR something was so funny it was fatal. Tone matters.
😭 Crying laughing face. Positive. Despite visual appearance, no one is upset.
🤔 Thinking face. Deep in thought, OR mild concern, OR passive disagreement.
🧐 Face with spiral eyes. Overwhelmed. Used when words are insufficient.
